Welcome Grey, your job is to take down the evil conglomerate known as NIS. Their main headquarter is situated in La Plas where they deploy their "security system" that governs our every day actions. As you know this system is a masquerade for citizens safety and instead allows the big brother to track every one of our movements. It's up to our revolution to bring the people their freedom. Once inside the main lobby, find a way to the main vault and then shut down the system! Unfortunately, the only intel we have is that this place is heavily guarded and what we are doing here is highly illegal! Go forth and bring the new age of freedom!
